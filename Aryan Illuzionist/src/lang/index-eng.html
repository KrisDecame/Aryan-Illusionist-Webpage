Show made for everyone...

Aryan is a stage and close-up illusionist from Croatia, always ready to entertain and amaze you with mental and visual effects, at any place or any time. His mission is to bring you a combination of magic, laugh and interaction that will, in the end, produce an unforgetable experience.

Pick a program, any program...

Stage Magic

The performance consists of small and big illusions, mental predictions and interactions with large audience, that will enjoy the elements of magic, surprise, mystique and comedy.

Close-up Magic

Magic act that happends in front of the spectator's, face to face. Illusionist is cruising through crowd and involves people into his act, using mostly everyday objects (cards, coins, sponge balls, etc.)

Magic for Kids

The main goal of this show is to bring laughs and excitement to children, by presenting them magical illusions adapted to their age. After the performance, children are offered extra entertainment: balloon modeling

Packages: 30 or 60 minutes.
